VR Skiing

VR Skiing Simulator

Role | Game Developer
Platform | PC
Date | 2024
Tools | Unity & Quest2

This is a Unity-developed VR skiing simulator, where players can enjoy the thrill of skiing from a first-person perspective.

Responsibilities

· Game Mechanics Development
Includes character actions, game UI, collectibles, and related systems.
· Game Concept Design
Focuses on creating the overall vision, theme, and aesthetic direction of the game.

Game Features | Map Signs and Collectibles

layers need to follow map signs to progress and collect various collectibles, such as coins and rings of different shapes, to earn higher scores.

Scores and collection progress are displayed in real-time on the UI interface.

Game Features | Camera Transition & Obstacles

In the game, players must avoid obstacles while striving to perform more high-difficulty special moves.

When players perform special moves, the camera automatically switches from a first-person perspective to a third-person view, allowing them to better see and appreciate the execution of their actions.

Choose A Map
Choose from a variety of level maps, including snow-capped mountains and deep seas.

Finish The Game
After completing a level, the results screen will display the number of rings and coins you collected, as well as the tricks and special moves you performed.
