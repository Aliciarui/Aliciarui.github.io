DEMO: Bliss

Semi-finished products made by UE4

This is a semi-finished indie action game demo with the theme of electricity called Bliss

You will play Aya, who comes to explore the scene, has been discovered by the witch since a long time ago. You need to survive the level and find the electric power.
Unlocking a part of the witch will restore power and allow players to enter a new level for adventure.

Platform | PC
Date | 2021
Tools | UE4(Blueprint & C++) - Maya - Blender - SubstancePainter - PhotoShop - AdobeIllustrator

Operation

WSAD or ↑↓←→: change the direction and move
SPACE: hold to jump
Right Button Click: attack

Purpose of the game demo

1 Combine puzzle solving with action games, giving players a complete gaming experience
2 Change game scenes and follow AI at any time, allowing players to get the excitement of the game

Level Design

I designed different gameplay in the Demo for the player to experience. Of course, the player needs to find the mechanism on the map to unlock the next part of the game. There are also mechanisms in the game that restrict the player's movement.

Dangerous Terrain

The player will first encounter the dangerous terrain. Once the player step on such terrain, he will be deducted (the lower left health bar is reduced). Therefore, he needs to move, jump, etc. to avoid the dangerous terrain.

Rotating Component

After entering the second stage, the player will see many rotating ray in the scene. The player still need to avoid such rays by moving, jumping, etc. Otherwise his blood will be deducted (the health bar in the lower left is reduced)

AI Tracking

While the player is trying to avoid obstacles, there is also an AI lightning in the scene. Once the player is found, the lightning will follow the player to move. If player stops moving, it will be caught up by the lightning.

Level Prop

Each time the player passes through a stage, he can unlock the corresponding level prop layer. After a certain period of time, he will return to the same game field. According to the design, there will be a puzzle-solving part around it, but it has not yet been completed.

Heart & Shield

There are a lot of props and obstacle designs in the game. In order to prevent the player from staying in the same position all the time, there is love-shaped props scattered in the scene, which means that the player can obtain by picking it up. There will also be power barriers around, and only after passing the corresponding stage can you enter the outer circle to look for Level Prop

Attack Witches

The player can find the attacking props in a specific position. Use the right mouse button to launch an electric ball to attack the witch (due to the barrier blocking it cannot attack directly). If the player has attacked 3 witches while avoiding the AI chase, the game wins.

HOW I design the game?

Special ideas

The Logo of TrashCan on the desktop
VS
The 3D scene of TrashCan for adventure

2D to 3D

The game scene is set in the computer screen. We must believe that there is another world in the computer screen. The 2D icon on the desktop will become a real scene model in the game. For example, there will be a recycle bin in the game, which corresponds to The logo of the trashcan on the desktop.

Specific Theme

Taking the trashcan mentioned above as an example, I extracted the logo mark and applied the concepts of "loop" and "transition" to the game scene and various parts, such as Level Prop and door decorations. This will make the game more complete and more like a whole.

Character Model & Animation Binding

I design and model all the characters and scene models, and draw them textures.
Since the whole story is related to electric magic, so I design an image of a wizard, wearing overalls similar to a clown and a wizard hat. I use MIXAMO to bind actions to the character model to make this level more vivid.

Hints in demo

It is important that we must give players enough hints to solve difficulties in the game.
For example, in the stage of avoiding dangerous terrain, it is impossible to let the dangerous part appear directly without buffer. The player will not have enough time to react. So I chose to highlight the target terrain first to give the player enough reaction time. Other prompts are also set up in the game, such as lighting up Level Prop layer by layer, suggesting the player's progress in the level and so on.

Other Design

BluePrint & C++

In the process of using UE4 to develop, I mainly use blueprints (character blueprints, level blueprints, etc.) Also I use C++ classes to implement asynchronous functions.

Spared Level Design Model

I have modeled the scene for the next level. This is an amusement park scene. The outer circle is a roller coaster. Players need to utilize props to solve puzzles and explore the whole scene, and finally leave this area in a hot air balloon.

Reference: Why Bliss?

You must be familiar with this wallpaper. It is the default wallpaper of Windows XP.
Behind this wallpaper, there is also a touching love story. I am attracted by such a love story. I want to make a game based on such a story. I believe that players can restore the original world through the wallpaper. This is why I designed such game with a 2D wallpaper and a 3D world conversion.
