CREATURE COUTURE

Vampire survivor-like action & fast fashion management

Role | Game Developer & Technical artist
Platform | PC
Date | 2024
Tools | Unity

In Creature Couture, taunt and trap fashion zombies and use their outdated looks to make new threads to satisfy the ever-growing consumers of fast fashion.

Travel across a post (fashion) disaster earth, where your fashion nightmares have all come to life! Bait jealous fashion zombies to steal their out-of-season clothes, and use the material to upcycle their outdated old clothes into haute couture! Set up your factory to make use of all the resources taken from fashion zombies and create bold new designs to sell to your discerning customer base. But watch out! Fashion moves fast, be sure to keep track of constant trend changes and adjust your output to avoid becoming a fashion disaster yourself!

Responsibilities

· Develop the overall gameplay mechanics.
· Create shaders to enhance the visual experience.

Area of Gameplay | The Wasteland

The goal in The Wasteland is for the Protagonist to trap as many Fisasters as possible and collect their clothing. The Protagonist runs around taunting enemies with their fashionable outfits, luring them toward Traps. When trapped, the Fisasters’ clothes are shredded, causing them to flee in embarrassment and drop pieces of clothing for you to collect.

There are different types of traps to choose from, each with unique functions.
For example, the glue trap covers a wide area and slows down enemies caught inside. It also damages their clothes, reducing the drop rate.

Change Traps
· Players can select the type of trap either by clicking with the mouse or using the Q and E keys on the keyboard.
· The number in the top-left corner indicates the remaining traps available.
· After selecting the desired trap, press the spacebar to place it.

Collect Drops
Drops are what enemies leave behind once they have been trapped. The Drops are used to fuel the machines in the factory and to keep them producing clothes to sell.

Area of Gameplay | Factory

The player can use the manhole that both the battlefield and the factory have to transfer between these two areas.
In the factory, the players can change different clothes based on the 'current trend' that will change during each session so be prepared. They can also change the clothes by adjusting the machines in the factory.

The factory operates automatically as long as there are sufficient materials.
Traps can be purchased in the shop using coins.

Change Clothes Style
The trend changes periodically, and players can adjust the machine's settings.
The closer the settings align with the trend, the more rewards players can earn.
